Christian Findlay is the Director of [Nimblesite](https://nimblesite.co), a Melbourne-based software development and DevEx consulting firm. He has over 20 years of software engineering experience, has created more than 10 open-source libraries, and specialises in Flutter, .NET, cloud architecture, and developer tooling. SharpLsp is his latest open-source project — an editor-agnostic .NET language server for C# and F#.

Find him at [christianfindlay.com](https://christianfindlay.com).
